Find the image that is different from the other two

There will be three images shown on the screen, one of them is very different from the other two.

Use 1,2 and 3 on the keyboard to select the one that you think is different from the other two.

For each set of images, you will have 2 seconds to response.

After selecting the image or timeout, press space to go into the next question.
