Find the image that is different from the other two

There will be three images shown on the screen, one of them is very different from the other two.

Use 1,2 and 3 on the keyboard to select the one that you think is different from the other two.

After you start, for each set of the images, they will show right at the place of the masks after 300ms, and would exist for 1000ms, then disappear. After that, you still have 1200ms to select the answer

You can select during image appearance, or after it disappear, but notice that you only have 2200ms in total to select your answer. After selecting the image or timeout, press space to go into the next question.

Before starting, you could try a few examples.
