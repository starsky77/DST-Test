Find the image that is different from the other two

In this test, you will be shown a series of three images on the screen. One of these images will be distinctly different from the other two. Your task is to identify this unique image.

Use the keys '1', '2', or '3' on your keyboard to select the image you believe is different from the other two.

After making your selection, press the 'Spacebar' to proceed to the next question.

Each set of images will be displayed for 800 ms. After this period, the images will disappear from the screen.

You have a total of 2000 ms (2 seconds) to make your selection.

You can try a few example sets to be familiar with the process before starting the actual test. The test examples can be very different from the real examples.
